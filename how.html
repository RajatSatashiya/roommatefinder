How it works??

1.

Connect with us!!!

Create an account and sign-in to our website. This helps us get connected with you and provide you with better room/roommate post recommendations.

2.

Browse through find Rooms page

Look at dozens of posts on find rooms page and chat with the owner on our site itself if you like a bungalow/apartment.

3.

Verification process begins!!!

In this process we verify whether the post put up by the host is real or not. Once, this process ends you can move on to the payments page.

4.

Payment Process

Once the payment is done, Roommate Finders gets 6% commission on the monthly rent decided by the owner while the host can still put up posts for free on our platform and also get their property a verified badge.
